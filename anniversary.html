Anniversary

Damian Lunny

There has been a Methodist society in Enniskillen for 238 years. The present church building was 150 years old in 2017 and so we give thanks to God and celebrate.

Anniversary booklets continuing the story of our last 50 years from 1967 to 2017 are available for sale from Friday 17th February. Contact us if you would like to have one.

You can download the Jubilee book (17.5Mb) which recounts our history up to 1917.

The Centenary book (24.5Mb) continues the story from 1917 to 1967.
