Welcome

Enniskillen Methodist Church, along with sister churches at Florencecourt, Letterbreen, Knockninny and Blacklion, form the Enniskillen Circuit. Enniskillen Methodist Circuit belongs to the Methodist Church in Ireland. You’re welcome to join us for the services and events that are listed. All activities which involve the physical meeting in groups and together for worship are currently suspended as part of our responsibilities in light of the Coronavirus pandemic. The church is not the building and we will be the church wherever we are. Keep up to date with the Methodist Church in Ireland’s Covid information which includes subpages on Church At Home, Covid-19 Stories, Prayer, Serving, World Church and more Resources.

They are there to encourage and equip us as church family for this present time and for what lies ahead.

The way we meet has changed for the time being. We are in touch via technology until the current crisis is over. This is what we would normally experience.

In Enniskillen, our Sunday morning service at 11:30am offers a creche and Children’s Church. Sunday School (Primary School aged children) and Bible Class (Years 8, 9 & 10) meets before the service at 10:30am. Throughout the week there are various groups that bring people of all ages together for friendship and growth in faith.
